School of Engineering Society
Far western University


The School of Engineering Society (SOES)) Club is dedicated to promoting the STEM field, specifically Civil & computer Engineering. We work to provide resources for any student, alumni, and professionals regardless of an individual's degree of knowledge or commitment in the field of Engineering. SOES meetings and hosts events that provide opportunities for networking, educational, and professional development related to computer & Civil Engineering. Our mission is to provide students with the skills, resources, motivation, and training necessary to become successful leaders in STEM.

About Us

We aim to help every individual grow as a person and as as tech professional to feel confident in their tech skills once they graduate. On top of that, we are a community of like-minded individuals who are eager to network and succeed. Come join us at the SOES Club at Far western University.

Our Aim

We are the students let club organiztion that believes in helping BCT and BCE students succeed in the STEM field. We offer events that are aimed and designed to grow your understanding of tech and fields related skills. Whether you're aiming to be a Software Engineer, Data Scientist, or Project Manager, we offer a community of like-minded individuals who are eager to connect and learn with others.
