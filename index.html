School of Engineering
Society

A community driven club with the aim of helping and providing civil & Computer Engineering students with all the necessary skills to succeed in their career. Every semester we make it our goal to have unique events for our club members. Come and join us for one of our exciting events!

Third semester Notes

SOES is About

We are the students that believes in helping BCT and BCE students succeed in the STEM field. We offer events that are aimed and designed to grow your understanding of tech and fields related skills.

Whether you're aiming to be a Software Engineer, Data Scientist, or Project Manager, we offer a community of like-minded individuals who are eager to connect and learn with others.

Events

Interested in learning Python, CSS/HTML/JS, or Swift? Then we are the club for you! We offer workshops for each of these programming languages and more, including Networking and Technical interview prep workshops. Whether you are a complete beginner or an advanced programmer, come join us at any of our events.

Resources

Are you not sure where to start or where to look for resources? We compiled a bunch of resources that we believe are best suited to help you out in this endeavor. These resources include information on Computer engineering classes at Far western university, Data Science, Web Development, getting started on a new language, and much more.

Collaborate

Here at the SOES, we want to include as many students as we can. For that reason we’ve decided to open up supportive members positions on our board to get fellow members more engaged with the club and, hopefully one day, into the Executive position. Come and checkout our open positions and apply.

About_Us

We aim to help every individual grow as a person and as as tech professional to feel confident in their tech skills once they graduate. On top of that, we are a community of like-minded individuals who are eager to have creative collaboration and succeed. Come join us at the SOES Society at Far western University.
